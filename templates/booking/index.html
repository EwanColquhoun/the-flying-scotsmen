The Flying Scotsmen

About Us

The Flying Scotsmen (TFS) were formed from a group of like-minded friends and amateur and professional flyers. TFS are based at Perth Scone Airfield (EGPT, PSL), Scotland. Created in 2010, the group started off with the trusty Cessna 172. In recent years the group has expanded and now has access to two group owned aircraft, a Glasair III and a Cessna 172. The aircraft are always hangered and have full and maintainance histories. We are now accepting new members!
