The Flying Scotsmen

About Us

The Flying Scotsmen (TFS) were formed from a group of like-minded friends who are amateur and professional flyers. TFS are based at Perth Scone Airfield (EGPT, PSL), Scotland. Created in 2010, the group started off with the trusty Cessna 172. In recent years the group has expanded and now has access to two group owned aircraft, a Glasair III and a Cessna 172.
The Glasair III is a fantastic aircraft for navigating around the country. Aerobatic, airways capable and with its exceptional performance it is a true touring machine. The aircraft are always hangered and have full and maintenance histories.
As a member and shareholder of the aircraft we require a monthly fee towards the upkeep of the aircraft. This fee covers the likes of planned maintenance, hanger costs and insurance. There is also an hourly rate. Information of all the costs associated can be requested using the contact form.
Before you can go flying we will need you to register. Once all the neccessary paperwork is completed we can then approved your booking.
